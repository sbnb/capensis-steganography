Email Steganography

Use Examples
============

Sending an email
----------------

Write secret text into field.
Choose from several images under field.
Encoded image is loaded into new tab.
Save as.

Then send email as normal, including encoded image.

Receiving an email
------------------

Save encoded image from received email.

Open steganography app.
Encoded image is loaded into program (how?).
Choose matching un-encoded image.
Image is decoded and secret message displayed.
